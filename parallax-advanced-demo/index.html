A Day in Spring

The flowers start to blossom, and everything just seems cute, because its spring! People are eating watermelon, wearing floral patterns, eating popsicles and just enjoying life. After a rough winter, its important to embrace the season change.

Even famous fast food stops can be a joy when out enjoying spring. Go ahead and order some animal fries and enjoy your milkshake in the warm spring sun.

Crystal shopping is one of my friend's favorite things to do in spring. Seriously, you should see how many crystals she has. A lot of crystals have different "healing" powers or can send positive energy out into the universe for you and support you during trying times. Check out your local crystal shop for some good vibes and see if anything catches your eye.

Don't be weary because it's dreary... Summer is right around the corner! I enjoy staying in and watching a movie or reading a book. And remember, April Showers bring May Flowers!
